Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

ArcTracker Bot ("we", "our", or "the Bot") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Discord bot.

2. Data Collection

ArcTracker Bot is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users.

Specifically:

  • We do not store user IDs, usernames, or messages.
  • We do not track user behavior or usage history.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

3. Data Usage

The Bot only processes the data provided in the slash commands (e.g., search queries) momentarily to generate a response. This data is processed in real-time and is not saved to any database or log file accessible by us.

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot operates on the Discord platform. By using the Bot, you are also subject to Discord's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Discord may collect data as described in their own policies.

5. Data Security

Since we do not store any user data, there is no risk of a data breach involving your personal information from our side.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our GitHub repository.
